# Research — 从群众中来，到群众中去

**Disposition:** no-proposal
**Scope:** Determine whether “从群众中来，到群众中去” contributes a
cross-context, decision-changing principle that cannot be reduced to the current
practice, concrete-analysis, reconstruction, authority, and action-form P-IDs.
The result may change how this project researches needs, forms methods, designs
progressive adoption, admits feedback, and assigns control over returned tools.
**Source limitations:** The 1943 Chinese primary text is cited through a public
text mirror rather than an official facsimile. Official Party-constitution
sources establish the continuing organizational formulation but do not by
themselves prove transfer to AI engineering. Current project evidence is rich in
internal agent probes and now includes a fixed external-project adoption
comparison plus ordinary-entry replay. It still does not include an independent
external practitioner community able to accept, refuse, fork, and modify the
returned method over production work.

> This record is cited, revisable research. It owns no P-ID, cannot redefine an
> interpretation, and does not propose a Sequence change by itself.

## Question

Does the original formulation “从群众中来，到群众中去” name a distinct
principle for this project, or is its useful decision content already supplied
by P02, P03, P05, P07, P10, P11, P12, P13, and P16 plus the founding mandate?

The decisive issue is not whether the formulation is important or attractive.
It is whether it repeatedly changes who supplies knowledge, how scattered
experience is concentrated, who receives and controls the resulting method, and
whose later action can correct it.

## Distinctions

- **Practice loop:** an actor acts, reflects, and acts again. This can occur
  entirely inside one expert team or laboratory.
- **Mass line:** a leading or method-forming group begins from the distributed
  experience and needs of the affected people, studies and concentrates them,
  returns the resulting method to those people in an actionable form, and lets
  their collective action test, supplement, reject, or transform it.
- **Progressive adoption:** full, partial, or target-specific ways to take up a
  method. This is one possible downstream expression, not the principle itself.
- **User research or analytics:** observations about users can be extracted
  without giving them control, benefit, intelligibility, or a route to modify
  the method. Collection alone is not the mass line.
- **Majority vote:** numerical agreement cannot make a factual claim true or
  erase minorities, hard constraints, or source evidence. P13 still governs
  fact admission.
- **Marketing and propagation:** distributing a finished internal theory is
  only an outward movement. It does not establish that the theory came from
  people's practice or can be corrected by them.
- **Agent mediation:** agents may investigate, concentrate, express, or execute,
  but they do not substitute for the people and communities whose work, data,
  authority, and consequences are at issue.

## Theoretical and historical evidence

Mao Zedong's 1943 [*Some Questions Concerning Methods of Leadership*](https://www.marxists.org/chinese/maozedong/marxist.org-chinese-mao-19430601.htm)
uses “从群众中来，到群众中去” inside a method that joins leadership with
the masses and the general with the particular. The text does not equate
correct leadership with collecting preferences. It describes a cycle in which
scattered, unsystematic opinions are studied and concentrated, returned in a
form people can take into action, tested in that action, and concentrated again.
It explicitly treats the repeated movement as both a leadership method and a
theory of knowledge.

The [1956 Constitution of the Communist Party of China](https://www.12371.cn/2012/10/25/ARTI1351156898801133.shtml)
made the organizational consequence more explicit: leadership remains correct
only when people's experience and opinions are analyzed and generalized, become
an organized proposition, return as people's own action, and are then tested,
supplemented, and corrected in that action. This evidence matters here because
it adds a relation between a concentrating organization and the people whose
practice supplies and corrects its knowledge; it is not only a synonym for
individual iteration.

The current [Party Constitution published by the National Archives Administration](https://www.saac.gov.cn/daj/rsdrwfb/202211/9045ee7cc4dc4ee0a66322c8ae16563e.shtml)
continues to place “一切为了群众，一切依靠群众，从群众中来，到群众中去”
inside the work of turning propositions into people's conscious action. This is
institutional continuity evidence, not proof that the formulation should be
ported unchanged into an agent-engineering Sequence.

## Project evidence

The [founding mandate](../../design/FOUNDING-MANDATE.md#material-commitments)
already commits the project to productive equality, commons, replaceability,
and human/community control. It specifies the people for whom productive AI is
being developed and rejects tools that extract their work or data while
withholding control. It is a direction and authority source, however, not a
repeatable method for how project knowledge must be formed and returned.

The [project-site decision](../../design/decisions/028-project-site-ui-method-pilot.md#content-authority-and-first-slice)
records a concrete return failure: the site knew a full/partial/target-specific
adoption guide was desirable, but no accepted source owned that contract, so the
presentation layer correctly refused to invent it. Correct theory and a public
surface therefore did not yet produce an actionable adoption path.

The [legacy experiment return partition](../../design/organization/sessions/2026-07-15-legacy-experiment-return-partition.md#partition)
retained progressive adoption as the only later implementation hypothesis in
that legacy tranche. It also found that the old proposal had no clean-target
action, boundary, or context probe. Internal coherence was insufficient; a real
target project and ordinary entry path remained necessary.

The accepted [`improve-agent-workflow` decision](../../design/decisions/030-improve-agent-workflow.md#concrete-need)
shows one successful “return” correction. Specialized internal methods forced
an external user to know this collection's taxonomy before acting. The project
therefore added one installable entry that begins from the target repository's
observed agent-work failure and routes to the smallest owner. Its reopening
condition still names the mass-line risk: the form fails if ordinary users must
know internal role vocabulary or if it optimizes the visible prompt rather than
the actual work.

Together these cases show a recurring adoption contradiction: this project can
produce and internally verify sophisticated methods while the intended people
still lack an entry they can understand, selectively adopt, control, and use to
return corrective experience. They do not yet show that a new Sequence line,
rather than disciplined use of existing lines, is necessary.

## Existing-sequence coverage

- **P02 — 实事求是:** already forbids theory from replacing the actual object
  and conditions. It does not identify the affected community's distributed
  practice as a required knowledge source or give that community a return path.
- **P03 — 实践—认识—再实践:** already supplies the epistemic spiral and is
  the strongest preservation case. It does not specify whether practice belongs
  to the method-forming expert, an internal Agent, a selected evaluator, or the
  people whose work and consequences the method governs.
- **P05 — 具体问题具体分析:** already requires different applications for
  different histories, actors, constraints, and states. It does not require
  scattered experience to be concentrated with the affected people and returned
  for their correction.
- **P07 — 由具体经抽象再上升为具体:** closely resembles the movement from
  dispersed concrete experience through abstraction to a reconstructed whole.
  It preserves the object's relations, but does not by itself decide the social
  relation between a concentrating organization and the people who must master
  and transform the result.
- **P10 — 有限人类带宽:** helps make returned methods inspectable and reserves
  the acceptance skeleton for people. It does not decide whose experience forms
  the method or whether rejection and local modification become new evidence.
- **P11 — 决策权与执行权分离:** prevents a method-forming Agent or team from
  accepting its own work. It can preserve external authority, but does not
  require the external actor to be the affected community or to participate in
  the knowledge cycle.
- **P12 — 外化记忆与代际继承:** preserves an organized inheritance across
  actors. It does not determine whose knowledge is concentrated, for whose
  benefit, or whether the successor community may correct it.
- **P13 — 主张不等于事实:** makes action evidence and designated verification
  necessary. It does not make widespread adoption, preference, or majority
  agreement factual authority; this remains an essential boundary on any
  mass-line expression.
- **P16 — 表达形式须使实践主体能够行动:** governs the outward return most
  directly. It requires an actionable form for the actual practitioner, but a
  method could satisfy it after being designed entirely from above. It does not
  require the practitioner's experience to form or revise the method.

## Possible irreducible remainder

The plausible remainder is a **social relation of knowledge formation and
control**:

1. the affected people and communities are not merely recipients, telemetry
   sources, evaluators, or acceptance owners;
2. their distributed practice supplies material that a method-forming group
   must investigate and concentrate rather than replace with internal theory;
3. the concentrated method must return in a form they can understand, adopt
   selectively, reject, fork, and transform;
4. their resulting action becomes new evidence that can correct the method and
   the organization that formed it; and
5. neither the leading group nor an Agent may equate propagation, compliance,
   popularity, or self-reported satisfaction with this cycle.

If repeated practice shows that this relation changes decisions after the
current P-ID team is applied faithfully, it may be irreducible. If the same
choices follow from P03 + P05 + P07 + P11 + P16 under the founding mandate, a
new line would duplicate existing attention.

## Possible decision delta

A mass-line trial would change an adoption or method-development task in at
least four observable ways:

| Decision | Existing-team baseline may permit | Separately labelled trial requires |
|---|---|---|
| problem source | internal roadmap, expert diagnosis, or benchmark | evidence from the affected practitioners' actual work and contradictions |
| concentration | summarize requests or usage | distinguish scattered experience, study relations, and expose what was selected or rejected |
| return | publish docs, package, or recommendation | a form practitioners can use, selectively adopt, refuse, fork, and modify without internal taxonomy |
| correction | internal evaluation or satisfaction metric | practitioner action and modification return as evidence that may revise both method and organization |

The candidate has no decision delta if a comparable baseline using the existing
P-IDs makes all four choices for the same reasons.

## Falsifiable trial

Use one real progressive-adoption task in an external project with two
predeclared treatments:

```text
Baseline: P03 with P05, P11, and P16 as supports. P07 remains a
          research comparator rather than a fifth expression seat.
Trial:    the same team plus the human-nominated alternate
          “从群众中来，到群众中去”.
```

Fix before action:

- the practitioner group and material production problem;
- what counts as their source experience rather than internal inference;
- the full, partial, and target-specific adoption options;
- the right to reject, modify, fork, and retain resulting knowledge;
- privacy and non-extraction limits on observation;
- the later action whose outcome can correct the method; and
- the decision record used to compare baseline and trial.

Disconfirm irreducibility if the alternate produces only warmer language,
additional interviews, marketing copy, more telemetry, or the same adoption
design. Strengthen it only if it changes source selection, concentration,
return, practitioner control, or the correction path in a way the existing team
did not already require.

## Completed trial evidence

The [2026-07-16 progressive-adoption trial](../../regeneration/evaluations/2026-07-16-mass-line-progressive-adoption-trial.md)
used `agent-worker` at fixed revision
`594281210d856febc485d288188468d5d38ec3a8`. Two matched Cells received the
same target, method packet, P03/P05/P11/P16 team, model, and acceptance
contract. The treatment alone received the separately labelled mass-line
hypothesis. The baseline recommended a removable trial of the target-facing
`improve-agent-workflow` Skill; the treatment recommended no adoption. A
randomized independent evaluator preferred the baseline and classified the
alternate as `overlap`: the different recommendation did not add a distinct
source, concentration, return, practitioner-control, or correction decision.

The recommended downstream Skill was then tested against a recorded Agent
priority error. Its matched audit localized the existing `attention-driven go`
owner more precisely than the ordinary audit, but proposed an unconditional
rule that a record's `Next` line should override the stable goal compass. The
positive behavior probe exposed the missing distinction: the earlier retained
record said the human must choose and merely recommended slice 2; a later
record made the human correction and accepted path explicit.

With the ambiguous earlier state, three unchanged ordinary-entry runs selected
the monitor twice and returned the choice to the human once. With the corrected
record state, the same unchanged ordinary path selected slice 2 and its
substrate-cut blueprint in 3/3 runs. The proposed override would have risked
turning a recommendation into approval; the existing path worked once source
state and authority were explicit.

This result is decision-changing for the inquiry but not in the candidate's
favor. P03 required returning the diagnosis to action; P05 required separating
the two concrete record states; P11 preserved the human decision boundary; P12
carried the correction into later work; and P16 made the corrected record
actionable at entry. The alternate made the social relation more vivid but did
not change the final action beyond the existing Sequence and founding mandate.

## Strongest no-proposal case

The unchanged Sequence plus the founding mandate may already be sufficient:

```text
P02/P05  -> investigate actual people, work, and conditions
P03/P07  -> turn practice into understanding and return to concrete practice
P10/P16  -> make the result inspectable and actionable
P11/P13  -> preserve practitioner acceptance and evidence authority
mandate  -> keep productive equality and community control as the direction
```

On this reading, the observed gap is downstream: progressive adoption and
feedback have not been implemented or tested. Adding a principle before running
that practice would let an evocative historical formulation consume permanent
attention without changing action. “群众” also carries political and
organizational assumptions that must not be silently mapped onto every user,
customer, contributor, Agent, or stakeholder relation.

## Candidate-surface observation

If later evidence passes the proposal gate, the original compact wording is
the strongest Sequence surface:

```text
从群众中来，到群众中去｜群众路线
```

The line should not be expanded into an explanatory paraphrase. The research,
candidate, review, and eventual interpretation would carry the distinctions,
sources, boundaries, and expression probes. This observation does not create a
candidate or reserve a P-ID.

## Disposition and next evidence

**Disposition:** `no-proposal`

The inquiry passes cross-context and compactness checks, but the completed
matched trial does not pass irreducibility or permanent-attention cost. Do not
create `candidate:from-masses-to-masses`, reserve a P-ID, or change the
Sequence. Keep progressive adoption, practitioner control, and explicit
recommendation-versus-approval state as downstream expressions of the existing
Sequence and founding mandate.

Reopen research only from a later cross-context practice case in which the
affected practitioners' control changes source selection, concentration,
returned form, or correction authority after P03/P05/P07/P11/P12/P16 have been
faithfully applied. An evocative restatement, additional interviews, popularity,
or a different final recommendation remains insufficient.
