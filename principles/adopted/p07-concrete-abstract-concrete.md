# Adopted revision — P07: Concrete, Abstraction, Concrete

**Status:** adopted revision
**Observed in:** Marx's method of political economy; reframing; source-preserving refactoring

## Candidate sequence line

## Recurrence

- In Karl Marx's *Grundrisse*, “The Method of Political Economy,” inquiry starts
  from the real and concrete, analytically resolves it into simpler
  determinations, then reconstructs the concrete in thought as a unity of
  determinations. The two movements belong to one method, not two unrelated
  workflows. Source: https://www.marxists.org/subject/dialectics/marx-engels/grundisse.htm
- `reframe` first extracts a domain's load-bearing abstract functions from its
  inherited surface, then reconstructs a new concrete product shape from the
  new paradigm's primitives.
- `article-refactor` first extracts source-bound information units into a
  neutral inventory, then builds a new argument skeleton and returns units to
  a concrete draft. Its existing method preserves provenance while changing
  form.

## Decision consequence

Before replacing an inherited skill corpus, record a source-linked inventory of
its concrete forms and then derive a neutral functional map. Treat current
names, command splits, directory conventions, and cross-skill layering as
hypotheses about form, not as the functions themselves. Recompose a new skill
only after the retained function, its user contradiction, its selected
principle expression, and its distinct decision gate are explicit.

## Existing-sequence check

P06 distinguishes essential relations from their appearance or contingent
form, but does not state the complete movement from an observed whole through
analysis to a reconstituted whole. P04 orders contradictions after their
structure is recognised; it cannot decide what must be abstracted from an
inherited form. The present P07 names only the ascending movement. This is a
clarifying revision of P07, not a request for a new P-ID.

## Counterexample / boundary

Abstraction is not arbitrary simplification, deletion of inconvenient detail,
or permission to discard history. Every abstraction must retain a source link
and must be tested by whether it can return to a more intelligible concrete
whole. P15 governs the scope of an actual transition after that analysis; it
does not replace the analysis.

## Expression probes

- A source-preserving writing refactor: inherited rhetorical order is separated
  from reusable claims and evidence before a new article is composed.
