## Scope and default

Review the working proposal
`P21｜让新生事物在受控实践中证明自己｜实践论 / 探索—利用` as a
general principle for innovation, exploration, and institutional renewal. The
default is to retain P01–P16 unless trial access supplies a durable choice that
cannot be made by P03, P08, P11, and P15 together.

The review refines “证明自己” to “保留受控实践的检验通道.” The former leans
toward confirmation; the latter names the proposed institutional decision while
allowing practice to reject the new thing.

## Team formation

- **Lead P03:** tests whether practice already entails giving an unqualified new
  proposal enough access to produce knowledge.
- **Standing liaison P08:** distinguishes a falsifiable claim from the
  institutional ability to run its disconfirming test.
- **Comparator P11 (self-reviewed):** separates lower-authority trial access from
  durable acceptance and tests whether that distinction already follows from
  separation of duties.
- **Preservation P15:** makes the strongest unchanged-sequence case and guards
  against a standing experiment budget or novelty bias.

P18 and P20 were supplied as evidence only. As candidates, they did not occupy
review-team seats.

## Team-member reports

### P03

- **Verdict:** insufficient evidence.
- **Decision delta:** the possible addition is an answer to evidence deadlock:
  whether a controller should provide a reversible, bounded, decidable practice
  entrance when qualification requires evidence but evidence requires access.
  P03 requires practice but does not allocate that entrance.
- **Overlap:** the current alternate protocol is already an expression of P03,
  constrained by P11 and P15. The candidate must still show that their combined
  use fails to produce the same choice.
- **Boundary:** practice is not an automatic entitlement for every novel claim.
  Admission to a trial still needs credible difference, safe scope, authority,
  and a result capable of defeating the proposal.
- **Unchanged alternative:** retain the alternate protocol as a project-level
  expression. Complete two independent real trials and one justified refusal or
  early termination before paying permanent Sequence attention.

### P08

- **Verdict:** support, not adoption.
- **Decision delta:** P08 can require a disconfirming test while the institution
  still refuses the access or resources needed to run it. P21 changes that
  decision by creating a bounded trial position without granting formal status.
- **Overlap:** P08 is necessary inside the candidate: without a disconfirming
  observation, “proving itself” becomes advocacy. It does not subsume the trial
  qualification, resource, and authority decision.
- **Boundary:** a trial must preserve the baseline, candidate delta,
  disconfirming observation, and sourced result. One passing trial grants no
  semantic or production authority, and exploration may not consume unbounded
  resources.
- **Unchanged alternative:** keep P21 as a governance mechanism until repeated
  cases show trial qualification still changes decisions after P03, P08, P11,
  P13, and P15 have been applied.

### P11

- **Verdict:** support with boundary; self-reviewed.
- **Decision delta:** P11 separates the actor running a trial from the authority
  that accepts a durable change. P21 adds a prior distinction between permission
  to generate evidence and qualification for durable use. Under P11 alone, an
  authority can remain properly separated while denying all unqualified action.
- **Overlap:** the alternate protocol uses P11 to prevent a candidate from
  accepting or promoting itself. That boundary makes P21 safe but does not by
  itself preserve an exploration lane.
- **Boundary:** trial access cannot disguise production, default, or semantic
  authority. Irreversible, high-risk, unethical, or non-consensual actions remain
  outside the candidate even when they could produce evidence.
- **Unchanged alternative:** derive bounded experiments case by case from P03,
  P08, P11, and P15. Add P21 only if those principles repeatedly leave a useful
  proposal in a no-evidence/no-access deadlock.

## Synthesis

The reports are not votes. P21 has a coherent cross-context decision delta:
**trial qualification is not formal qualification**. It addresses the evidence
deadlock in which an alternative cannot qualify without evidence and cannot
produce evidence without already qualifying. Agent-worker's measurement-only
A/B route, Sikong's route-only evaluation, and principle-cultivation's alternate
seat show that a lower-authority evidence lane is a reusable form rather than one
repository convention.

The theoretical roots separate cleanly:

- *On Practice* establishes that knowledge must arise from and return to
  practice, but does not allocate trial access.
- March explains why exploitation of known certainties tends to crowd out
  uncertain exploration, supplying the organizational problem P21 addresses.
- The [*Communist Manifesto*, Chapter I](https://www.marxists.org/archive/marx/works/1848/communist-manifesto/ch01.htm)
  shows that relations which once developed productive forces can later become
  fetters. It explains institutional rigidity, not a trial protocol.
- [“Science and technology constitute a primary productive force”](https://www.moe.gov.cn/jyb_xwfb/xw_zt/moe_357/s3579/moe_90/tnull_3104.html)
  and the [“Three Represents”](https://english.scio.gov.cn/featured/chinakeywords/2018-03/16/content_50715219.htm)
  identify valuable developmental direction and organizational alignment. They
  do not solve trial access, falsification, budget, or promotion; they are
  adjacent political expressions rather than P21's general root.

The candidate nevertheless fails the present admission-cost test. The repository
has a trial protocol and examples of evaluation lanes, but no completed P20 work
trial and no comparative case in which the existing P03/P08/P11/P15 team withheld
a useful experiment that P21 alone enabled. The forward probe verifies that the
protocol is understandable; without a baseline it cannot establish the
candidate's independent effect.

Retain P21 as an incubating candidate with the revised line:

`P21｜为新生事物保留受控实践的检验通道｜实践论 / 探索—利用`

Reopen adoption only after evidence includes:

1. two independent real-work contexts in which trial access was the live choice;
2. a recorded baseline under P03, P08, P11, and P15 and a different P21 choice;
3. at least one refused or terminated trial that demonstrates the boundary; and
4. a result showing that P21 changed a decision rather than merely renaming the
   existing protocol.

## Participation recommendation

Eligible for a bounded alternate trial only in a future architecture or routing
evaluation where an unproven route would otherwise lack an evidence path. Record
whether P03/P08/P11/P15 already choose the same measurement-only or route-only
lane; if they do, the outcome is `overlap`. Fix budget and exit in advance, grant
no production/default authority, and do not place P20 or another alternate in the
same activation. This is eligibility evidence, not nomination or adoption.
