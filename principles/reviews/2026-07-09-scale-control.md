## Synthesis

P17 fails the irreducibility gate. Its valuable test has been added to P15’s
expression probes: before adding or merging a control structure, map disturbances
to response, verification, and recovery paths, then retain only distinctions an
existing path cannot preserve.

P18 remains a strong incubation candidate. Ashby supplies a clear universal
root and P04/P05/P11 identify a distinct possible decision delta. P15’s dissent
is decisive for now: the record lacks repeated cases where P11, P13, P14, and
P15 are all correctly applied but the controller still fails for lack of
distinguishable response capability. Collect those cases before seeking adoption.
