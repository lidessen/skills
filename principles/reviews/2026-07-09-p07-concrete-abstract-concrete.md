“由具体经抽象再上升为具体.” The default is to retain the present line and
clarify only the P07 interpretation if that preserves the same decision.

names and command boundaries are concrete historical forms. Before selecting a
new architecture, the work must distinguish those forms from durable functions

## Synthesis

The review finds a real distinction: P06 identifies surface versus structure;
P04 selects a leading tension after the situation has been made intelligible;
P15 limits the resulting intervention. None names the complete method of
starting from an actual concrete, analytically deriving determinations, and
returning to a reconstituted concrete. That method recurs in both `reframe` and
`article-refactor`, and now governs the collection's regeneration.

The strongest unchanged-sequence case is also credible: the present P07 plus a
clearer interpretation might suffice. The proposed line wins only if the
human judges that the omitted descending movement is a recurring source of
wrong decisions, not merely a more elegant philosophical formulation.
