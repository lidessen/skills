# P03 — Interpretation

**Sequence source:** P03｜实践—认识—再实践｜实践论

> Read P03 in `principles/SEQUENCE.md` first.

## Shared reading

Knowledge is neither a static reading nor raw activity. Practice produces a
testable encounter, reflection reorganizes understanding, and a later practice
can test that revised understanding.

## Decision questions

- What practice could test or revise the current understanding?
- What later practice could test the understanding produced here?

## Boundary and common misreading

Iteration is not motion for its own sake. P03 does not itself decide the
priority or scope of the next action.

## Theoretical roots

- 毛泽东，《实践论》 — the relation between knowing and doing.

## Expression probes

- `principle-cultivation` turns project evidence into candidates, reviews, and
  revised future practice.
- A regression test converts a discovered failure into a durable future check.
- Moniro’s error–reflection–understanding spiral is a later project expression.

## Revision discipline

Revise from a failed expression probe, counterexample, or corrected source root.
Clarify the loop’s stages and handoff. A separate fact-admission rule belongs to
P13, not to P03.
