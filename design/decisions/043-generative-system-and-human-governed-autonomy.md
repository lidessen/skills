# 043 — Generative System and Human-Governed Autonomy

**Status:** accepted design; autonomous operating transition not yet implemented
**Date:** 2026-07-20
**Approved by:** principal

## Concrete pressure

The repository already distinguishes its Principle Sequence, interpretations,
method Skills, domain methods, cognition, Work Cells, orchestration, formal
operations, and human acceptance. The pieces have explicit local boundaries,
but their whole generative relation remained implicit. A reader could recover
what each module owns without recovering how reality becomes principle,
principle becomes method, method becomes situated organization, and practice
returns evidence capable of changing later understanding.

The accepted formal operating mode is also human-initiated. That is the correct
current safety boundary, but it no longer states the whole desired direction:
the project should eventually operate as an agent-composed adaptive system that
a person can guide, inspect, interrupt, and correct without manually recreating
every task and handoff. Treating that goal as merely “add a scheduler” would
give execution mechanics an authority they do not own. Treating it as a new
Skill would hide a project-wide design and governance decision inside one
optional method.

## Form decision

Adopt one project design decision and one concise projection in
[`design/DESIGN.md`](../DESIGN.md). The public READMEs may link to that accepted
model, but remain explanatory projections.

Do not add a Sequence entry, Skill, resident manager Agent, autonomous queue,
or runtime schema in this decision. The model is a project-specific synthesis
of the existing Sequence and accepted architecture. Its future runtime
transition requires a separate Strategy Case, implementation decision, and
representative operating evidence.

## Sequence grounding

No new P-ID is required to carry this design:

- **P02** keeps “道” outside the repository as objective reality and material
  conditions rather than a project-owned metaphysical source;
- **P03** supplies the return from effects through practice, renewed cognition,
  and changed later practice;
- **P07** carries the movement from concrete experience through abstract
  principle and method to a newly formed concrete situation;
- **P04** makes the present principal contradiction select the live formation;
- **P11 / P13** separate proposal, execution, verification, fact admission, and
  durable commitment; and
- **P14** keeps rebuildable runtime views and state projections from acquiring
  source or fact authority merely because they are convenient; and
- **P15** prevents the generative model from becoming a mandatory hierarchy or
  universal orchestration pipeline.

The pending [bounded-autonomy candidate](../../principles/candidates/bounded-autonomy.md)
remains non-authoritative. This model provides a concrete multi-actor setting
for a later comparison, but no matched operating trial yet shows that P23
changes a decision beyond the existing P11/P13/P15 boundary. Conceptual fit is
not adoption evidence.

## The generative relation

The model borrows the generative movement expressed by the [received *Dao De
Jing*](https://ctext.org/dao-de-jing/zh), rather than treating the text as a
literal software architecture or importing every historical claim as project
doctrine:

```text
道 → 一 → 二 → 三 → 万物
                     │
                     └── practice evidence ──► renewed understanding
```

The decisive term is **生**: each transition must say how one level produces a
more concrete level without transferring authority that the source did not
grant.

| Movement | Project meaning | Authority boundary |
|---|---|---|
| **道** | objective reality, material conditions, inherited human knowledge, and practice outside the repository | the project does not own reality or truth; it can only encounter, investigate, and revise toward them |
| **一** | [`principles/SEQUENCE.md`](../../principles/SEQUENCE.md), the compact and fallible semantic root | only explicit human adoption changes it |
| **Unfolding of One (一的展开)** | source-bound interpretations that make one P-ID usable and less drift-prone | an interpretation is a licensed derivative, not a second canon or an equal semantic source |
| **二** | domain-general methodology Skills formed by selecting and expressing principles | a Skill teaches a recurring judgment; it does not acquire project facts, execution, or acceptance authority |
| **三** | a live formation produced by a selected method, concrete domain material, and the present principal contradiction | a domain Skill is reusable professional capacity; the concrete “three” exists only when situation, evidence, actors, bounds, and acceptance are formed for actual work |
| **万物** | Work Cell and Swarm attempts, code, documents, decisions, products, and effects in practice | outputs remain claims or effects until the owning verifier and acceptance path settle them |

This is not a one-way taxonomy. Practice outcomes return through traceable
observation, verification, cognition, and reflection. They may revise a task,
formation, Skill, or accepted design. Only evidence that survives principle
research, candidate review, and human adoption may revise the Sequence. No
runtime output automatically rewrites its own governing source.

## Governance, administration, and execution

The “party–government” analogy is accepted as a conceptual aid for separating
political direction from administration. It is not a vocabulary requirement
for portable Skills, runtime types, or public adopters.

The corresponding project structure is:

| Plane | Composition and role | Cannot do |
|---|---|---|
| **Human-led governance** | the principal or future accountable community, the founding mandate, the Sequence, accepted design, and human-approved strategic decisions establish purpose and durable direction; Agents may research and advise | an artifact cannot become a political subject; an Agent recommendation cannot manufacture consent, doctrine, or final authority |
| **Administrative operation** | conditional operating seats sense signals, prepare strategy and estimates, admit bounded missions, maintain continuity, coordinate verification, integrate accepted work, and route correction | it cannot redefine purpose, enlarge its own authority or budget, or turn task intake into automatic acceptance |
| **Execution and production** | differentiated Work Cells use prepared context, Skills, tools, models, and bounded workspaces; orchestration supplies direct, Swarm, queue, or later execution forms | a Cell or orchestration kernel cannot invent semantic work, accept its own result, schedule itself from model-shaped text, or merge durable effects |
| **Verification and settlement** | independent evidence paths and designated human or host owners test claims, accept residual risk, and commit effects | verification is not subordinate self-certification, and agreement or vote count does not make a claim true |

Skills are methods used by these actors; they are not themselves members of a
governing body. Domain Skills resemble reusable professional capacities inside
the administrative and productive system. A Work Cell is the minimum
differentiable labor unit, not the whole government and not an authority over
the organization that prepared it.

## Autonomous operation without autonomous sovereignty

The target is **operational autonomy under human governance**, not a system
that grants itself sovereign authority. A mature system may, within an
explicitly accepted envelope:

- sense retained external events, failures, drift, and phase changes;
- refresh source-linked cognition and propose a principal contradiction;
- prepare strategies, estimates, missions, and temporary formations;
- admit and execute already-authorized classes of work through Work Cells and
  orchestration;
- obtain independent verification, contain failures, recover, and submit
  reviewable effects; and
- return observed outcomes to cognition, practice correction, Skill revision,
  design review, or principle inquiry.

It may not merely because it is autonomous:

- change the founding mandate or Sequence;
- make itself the representative of users, practitioners, or affected
  communities;
- enlarge its own permissions, resource envelope, or allowed effect scope;
- convert its own output, telemetry, consensus, or successful terminal call
  into accepted fact;
- accept material residual risk, merge a consequential change, or make an
  irreversible external commitment without the designated authority; or
- hide a loss of control behind a provider, scheduler, memory store, or central
  Agent.

This produces one bounded control loop:

```text
retained signal
      ↓
source-linked cognition
      ↓
contradiction and strategy proposal
      ↓
human approval or a previously approved policy envelope
      ↓
mission formation and task shaping
      ↓
Work Cell orchestration and bounded effects
      ↓
independent verification and settlement
      ↓
practice evidence, correction, and renewed cognition
```

The durable center of this loop should be inspectable sources, state
transitions, authority envelopes, and recovery paths—not one omniscient
resident Agent. Agents occupy temporary roles. State and evidence survive role
replacement.

## Relation to current operation

The repository currently implements **human-initiated bounded adaptation**, not
continuous autonomous operation. [Decision 012](012-bounded-adaptive-organization.md)
already supplies conditional formation and correction boundaries;
[Decision 015](015-human-initiated-formal-operations.md) keeps the active trigger
human-authorized; and [Decision 031](031-extensible-work-cell-orchestration.md)
supplies multi-Cell execution mechanics without semantic scheduling authority.

This decision changes the desired long direction and therefore creates a
legitimate reopening condition for the human-only trigger boundary. It does not
silently repeal that boundary. Until a later transition is approved and
verified, schedules, Issues, comments, webhooks, model suggestions, and queue
submissions remain non-authoritative intake proposals.

## Smallest future transition

The next step is a Strategy Case for **supervised autonomy**, followed by one
bounded operating pilot. The case must decide, from real work:

1. which retained signals may propose work;
2. which low-consequence work classes, if any, have a pre-approved execution
   envelope;
3. which durable source owns mission eligibility and recoverable state;
4. where human approval, independent verification, pause, takeover, rollback,
   and budget escalation occur;
5. how project cognition informs a proposal without becoming task or fact
   authority; and
6. what observation would show that autonomous operation adds ceremony,
   correlated error, or unsafe authority rather than useful continuity.

The first pilot may autonomously produce a proposal, evidence packet, branch,
or pull request. It must not autonomously merge, amend the Sequence, expand its
envelope, or claim that one clean run proves a reliable autonomous system.

## Acceptance and reopening

This design is present when a later reader can distinguish the semantic root,
method expressions, live formation, execution, empirical return path, and the
four authority planes without treating them as a single hierarchy.

Reopen it if practice shows that the `道 → 一 → 二 → 三 → 万物` relation cannot decide a
real ownership or formation question; if the political analogy causes portable
interfaces to lose generality; if supervised autonomy cannot operate without a
central Agent acquiring semantic or acceptance authority; or if actual users
cannot understand, interrupt, refuse, or recover the system's actions.
