## Context

The collection already has specialized, deliberately non-central methods:
[`practice-cycle`](../../skills/practice-cycle/SKILL.md) settles or routes an
observed practice; [`form-guidance`](../../skills/form-guidance/SKILL.md)
selects a carrier and stops; [`strategic-advisory`](../../skills/strategic-advisory/SKILL.md)
prepares advice only; and [`artifact-organization`](../../skills/artifact-organization/SKILL.md)
audits authority-and-lifetime layout. [Work Cell](../../packages/work-cell/README.md)
supplies bounded execution evidence without a board, scheduler, shared memory,
or central semantic router.

These boundaries avoid a false central agent, but they leave an organizational
question implicit: after an observed disturbance, what selects the relevant
role, what crosses the handoff, and what may change the organization itself?
The [organization audit](../organization/sessions/2026-07-10-operating-model.md) finds
that this is a repeated decision and authority gap, not a folder-cleanup issue.

## Decision

Adopt an **Organization Operating Model** as a durable part of the accepted
design. It is a human-governed coordination contract, not a semantic source,
skill, runtime, task board, or permanent management agent.

The model has four layers:

Each layer has a distinct authority. The Sequence remains the only semantic
root; an organization model may not redefine P-IDs. Skills teach judgments but
do not commit shared facts. Work Cells and campaigns produce evidence but do
not accept their own result. Views, indexes, and summaries are projections
unless explicitly promoted through a separate governed source.

### Coordination contract

The design must name, for every standing role, its activation evidence, output,
receiving owner, and authority boundary. The normal route is conditional, never
a mandatory pipeline:

| Disturbance or need | Select only this role when | Output crosses to | It cannot do |
|---|---|---|---|
| ordinary bounded work | no observed learning or form contradiction | designated implementation/verifier | manufacture a cycle or plan |
| observed practice must change a later judgment | `practice-cycle` has a real result and a disconfirming observation | settle, next practice, or named domain owner | accept durable work or become planner |
| recurring action lacks a truthful carrier | `form-guidance` finds a form contradiction | selected carrier's content owner | define that owner's doctrine or workflow |
| phase evidence requires a human multi-horizon choice | `strategic-advisory` has settled evidence or changed conditions | proposed Strategy Case and named human | commit missions, resources, or a queue |
| agent repeatedly fails a bounded judgment | `skill-engineering` has a recurring action gap | behavior-tested skill expression | own project facts or acceptance |
| artifact authority, lifetime, or reconstruction conflicts with design | `artifact-organization` finds a material carrier gap | one transition and host design | coordinate live work or define a domain method |
| a P-ID meaning may be insufficient | `principle-cultivation` has source-bound inquiry | research/candidate/review and human | alter the Sequence itself |
| bounded execution/evaluation is required | Work Cell has explicit intent, acceptance, and capability bounds | traceable run record and designated reviewer | infer authority, schedule work, or adopt doctrine |

Every handoff carries: the triggering observation, source/status of evidence,
unresolved decision, receiving owner, verifier/committer when the result is
durable, and the observation that would reopen the route. A handoff that omits
one of these is a claim, not organizational settlement.

### Sensing, adaptation, and self-organization

The system is **self-sensing** only through retained evidence, not an agent's
self-description. Signals include a route repeatedly rejected by its boundary,
handoff information repeatedly missing, an execution result that cannot settle
the promised decision, context that omits a governing constraint, or a material
source/projection conflict.

It is **self-adaptive** when a signal selects the smallest existing response:
practice evidence routes to `practice-cycle`; a carrier mismatch to
`form-guidance`; expression failure to `skill-engineering`; context failure to
`harness`; authority/lifetime drift to `artifact-organization`; phase direction
to `strategic-advisory`; and possible semantic insufficiency to
`principle-cultivation`. One observation is evidence for a probe, not an
automatic amendment.

It is **self-organizing** only in the bounded sense that a live contradiction
selects a temporary formation of relevant roles. There is no standing
orchestrator, required skill order, universal preflight, autonomous task queue,
or automatic rewrite of this model. A durable change to role boundaries,
authority, or this contract requires reviewable evidence and human approval.

## Sequence expression

- **P04:** the live contradiction, rather than a fixed process, selects the
  temporary formation.
- **P09:** slow constraints, activated methods, and volatile evidence remain
  layered rather than being loaded as one permanent controller.
- **P11 / P13:** execution, verification, commitment, and fact admission remain
  distinct and traceable.
- **P12 / P14:** decision-changing handoffs survive in named records, while
  rebuildable views do not gain authority.
- **P15:** begin with this contract, not a new coordinator; add a distinct form
  only when a repeated gap has no existing owner.

## Alternatives rejected

| Alternative | Why it is not selected now |
|---|---|
| Extend `artifact-organization` into a general coordinator | It would collapse its carrier-layout gate with live routing and domain methods. |
| Create `organizational-guidance` immediately | The evidence shows a missing durable contract, not yet a recurring agent judgment that a separate skill must teach. |
| Add a scheduler, board, shared memory, or central agent | Existing Work Cell and strategy boundaries explicitly reject these authorities; a document and conditional routes meet the current need. |
| Keep coordination implicit | It leaves repeated handoff and authority decisions to reconstruction from chat and isolated prompts. |

## Acceptance

- `design/DESIGN.md` carries the stable organization contract without creating
  a second semantic source.
- A route matrix makes each standing role's input, output, receiver, and
  non-authority inspectable.
- An action probe routes actual phase evidence through only the relevant roles
  and preserves source status, human approval, and Work Cell limits.
- A boundary probe rejects a request for a universal pipeline or automatic
  amendment and keeps `artifact-organization` limited to carrier layout.
- The organization campaign is settled and archived; no permanent process
  headquarters survives it.
