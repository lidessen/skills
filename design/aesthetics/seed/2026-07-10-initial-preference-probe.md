# Initial Aesthetic Preference Probe

**Status:** observed — provisional seed
**Authority:** [decision 017](../../decisions/017-aesthetic-practice-pilot.md)

## Object

Establish a first, explicitly provisional preference seed for the project's
future identity, documents, UI, and illustration. This seed is a
conversation with the Principal, not a claim about general beauty or a finished
design direction.

## Comparative directions

| Key | Direction | Relation made visible | Visual vocabulary | Risk to test |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| A | **Common Workshop / 公共工坊** | accessible shared production, repair, and practical intelligence | editorial grid; warm paper/charcoal; tool marks; restrained vermilion; legible type | may feel too utilitarian or nostalgic |
| B | **Constructive Commons / 构成公域** | collective force, open infrastructure, and deliberate construction | bold geometry; high-contrast red/black/cream; asymmetry; structural typography | may become historical-poster pastiche or visually coercive |
| C | **Living Technology / 生长的技术** | intelligence embedded in life, care, and ecological continuity | deep indigo/earth; organic textures; calm light; spacious rhythm | may lose productive precision or become generic “nature tech” |

## Seed test

The visual probe places the three directions beside the same imaginary project
surface. The Principal may choose one, rank them, combine named dimensions, or
reject all three. The response is recorded as a preference signal with the
reason and contrast; it does not automatically choose colors, components, or
an illustration style for all work.

## Principal observation

The Principal liked the design language of all three panels and preferred **A
as the content anchor**: concrete shared production, repair, tools, and
human-scale work should lead the project's subject matter. The preference is
not a full selection of A's visual vocabulary.

The provisional synthesis is:

| Dimension | Seed to carry forward | Source panel |
|---|---|---|
| Narrative subject | workshop, repair, accessible making, concrete tools and work | A — Common Workshop |
| Composition | confident geometry, structural hierarchy, controlled asymmetry | B — Constructive Commons |
| Material and atmosphere | warmth, tactility, life, calm depth, and space for reflection | C — Living Technology |

The generated three-panel reference is retained at
[`references/2026-07-10-initial-triptych.png`](../references/2026-07-10-initial-triptych.png).
It remains a seed artifact, not a reusable public direction or final asset.

## Disconfirming observation

The seed is insufficient if the Principal cannot distinguish the directions,
finds all three unrelated to the project's social/production relation, or later
real artifacts require a different contrast to make the work intelligible.
