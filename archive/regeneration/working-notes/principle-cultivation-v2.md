# Principle Cultivation V2 — Regeneration Record

**Status:** in progress
**Lineage source:** host `principles/SEQUENCE.md`, P01–P16

## Target action gap

An agent can currently turn any interesting source material directly into a
candidate. It cannot preserve a cited inquiry that reaches `no-proposal`, and a
later agent cannot distinguish open research from a live request to change the
Sequence.

## Retained seed

- One citation-free semantic source; stable P-IDs and one interpretation path.
- Practice produces evidence; research precedes a candidate, and preservation is
  the default when the six candidate gates do not hold.
- A selected existing-P-ID committee supplies evidence, never a vote or adoption
  authority; direct human adoption still receives retrospective review.
- Candidate, adopted evidence, interpretation, Sequence, and downstream
  expression remain separate; reconciliation is selective.
- A human-nominated alternate remains outside lineage and is tested through a
  baseline, delta, disconfirming observation, and sourced result.

## Acceptance observations

- A durable inquiry reaches `no-proposal` without creating a candidate.
- Re-running `propose` on one research note updates one linked candidate.
- Legacy `extract` remains candidate-producing when the gate passes.
- Existing interpretation, review, alternate, adoption, and Sequence boundaries
  remain available and do not require the full research layer by default.
