## Inventory invariants

- A source skill remains readable evidence until a human-approved migration or
  retirement decision says otherwise.
- “Legacy form” means a currently contingent expression, not an error or a
  deletion instruction.
- Current Primary/Supporting assignments are recorded provenance, not a verdict
  on the regenerated architecture.
