# Exciting Changes to Pediatric Care

Northside Community Clinic is thrilled to announce an innovative new approach
that puts families first. Starting August 5, we will reserve 12 same-day
appointment slots each weekday for urgent pediatric cases.

## Key Benefits

- Fast and seamless access
- A family-centered experience
- Flexible online options

Caregivers can call the clinic to request a slot. A triage nurse uses the
existing triage protocol to decide whether a case is urgent. Front-desk staff
do not make this decision.

## Looking Ahead

Unused slots are released online at 14:00 for next-day appointments. This
six-week trial will measure pediatric waiting time and abandoned calls. It does
not add clinicians or increase total appointment capacity. In the previous four
weeks, 18 caregivers calling about a sick child were told that no same-day
appointment was available.

Together, these changes mark an important step toward a brighter future for
children and families.
