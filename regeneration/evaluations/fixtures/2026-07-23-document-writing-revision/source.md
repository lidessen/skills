# Confirmed clinic notice facts

- Starting August 5, Northside Community Clinic will reserve 12 same-day
  appointment slots each weekday for urgent pediatric cases.
- A caregiver may request a same-day slot by calling the clinic. No app or
  online account is required.
- A triage nurse decides whether a case is urgent under the clinic's existing
  triage protocol. Front-desk staff do not make that decision.
- Reserved slots not used that day are released to online booking at 14:00 for
  next-day appointments.
- The change is a six-week trial.
- During the trial, the clinic will measure pediatric waiting time and abandoned
  calls.
- The trial does not add clinicians or increase the clinic's total appointment
  capacity.
- In the previous four weeks, 18 caregivers who called for a sick child were
  told that no same-day appointment was available.

Do not claim that the trial guarantees an appointment, reduces emergency-room
use, improves outcomes, or will continue after six weeks.
