## Verdict and limits

This supports the action, boundary, authority, and context claims in
[decision 013](../../design/decisions/013-naming-and-articulation.md). It does
not prove that the method improves naming quality generally: there is no
matched baseline or independent evaluator, and the selected term already
appeared in decision 012. Human adoption of the Skill and of the organization
term remains separate from this behavior evidence.
