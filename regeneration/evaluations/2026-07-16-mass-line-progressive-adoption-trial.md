# Mass-Line Progressive-Adoption Trial

**Date:** 2026-07-16

**Subject:** `agent-worker` at fixed revision
`594281210d856febc485d288188468d5d38ec3a8`

**Model:** `deepseek-v4-flash`, non-thinking mode

**Status:** alternate treatment overlapped; no Sequence proposal supported

## Question

The linked [research inquiry](../../principles/research/from-masses-to-masses.md)
asks whether “从群众中来，到群众中去” changes method-formation and adoption
decisions beyond P03 with P05, P11, and P16. The test used a fixed external
project packet rather than this collection's own organization and separated two
questions:

1. Does the alternate change a progressive-adoption recommendation?
2. Does the smallest recommendation that survives comparison improve a real
   Agent action through the target's ordinary entry path?

The target was selected because its
[`goals/record.md` at the fixed revision](https://github.com/lidessen/agent-worker/blob/594281210d856febc485d288188468d5d38ec3a8/goals/record.md)
preserves a concrete human correction: an Agent named the long-term monitor as
the active initiative although the immediate path was decision 006 slice 2.
Repository records evidence recorded practice; they do not stand in for
unrecorded maintainers, users, or a wider practitioner community.

## Phase 1: matched adoption proposals

acceptance contract, model, and budget. The treatment Cell alone received the

The baseline found a bounded decision not already owned by the target's
`attention-driven` method: when an Agent-work failure crosses Skill, context,
tool, verification, and handoff surfaces, an external diagnostic entry can
locate the smallest owner. It recommended the already accepted,
self-contained [`improve-agent-workflow`](../../skills/improve-agent-workflow/SKILL.md)
as a removable first trial, not full adoption of this collection.

The alternate instead emphasized practitioner control and concluded that the
target's existing practice already satisfied the treatment. That changed the
final recommendation, but the independent evaluator found no distinct change
to source selection, concentration, returned form, practitioner control, or
correction authority. The treatment supplied a more conservative reading, not
an irreducible decision consequence.

The installed Skill materially improved the report's owner localization: it
separated stable goal truth, immediate path, context delivery, reusable
guidance, capability, verification, and handoff, then kept the proposed change
inside the existing `go` command. The evaluator preferred that report.

That preference did not settle the action. Both reports still inferred a
runtime correction before the ordinary-path probes had run. The preferred
report proposed that a record's `Next` line override the goal compass when they
differ. The retained pre-correction state, however, said that the human must
choose between alternatives and merely recommended one. Treating that
recommendation as an accepted path would cross the authority boundary the
diagnosis was meant to protect.

durable source expressed that state and authority clearly, no extra entry
mechanism was needed.

## Revised conclusions

### Principle inquiry

The alternate failed the irreducibility gate. P03 already required returning
the proposal to action; P05 required distinguishing the two concrete record
states; P11 prevented an Agent from converting a recommendation into human
approval; P12 preserved the correction in durable path memory; and P16 required
an entry form from which the next Agent could act. The founding mandate still
supplies the project's direction toward community control. The mass-line phrase
made that relation vivid but did not change the final decision beyond those
existing sources.

Disposition: **`no-proposal`**. Do not create a candidate, reserve a P-ID, or
change the Sequence. A later cross-context case may reopen research only if the
affected practitioners' control changes source selection, concentration,
returned form, or correction authority after the existing P-IDs have been
faithfully applied.

### Progressive adoption

The external diagnostic Skill showed bounded value: it produced a more precise
owner model in the matched audit. It did not earn mandatory or default use, and
its first action recommendation did not survive behavior testing. Retain its
current role as an optional diagnostic entry when the owner is unclear; verify
through the target's ordinary path before changing the owner. This is consistent
with its accepted [smallest-form decision](../../design/decisions/030-improve-agent-workflow.md#smallest-truthful-form).

### Project-form lesson

Do not turn “read the latest `Next`” into a mechanical priority rule. A durable
continuity source must distinguish:

- observed current state;
- recommendation;
- pending human choice;
- accepted immediate path; and
- the next observable move.

The mechanism can deliver that source, but it cannot manufacture the authority
transition. When the source is explicit, the current ordinary entry method is
sufficient in this case.

## Cost and limitations

Twelve successful Cells used 3,981,899 tokens, approximately $0.52785135 at the
recorded 2026-07-10 price revision, and 532,441 ms of summed Cell duration.
Pre-model configuration and capability mismatches used zero tokens and are not
included.

The trial used one repository, one model family, repository-recorded practice,
and synthetic replay of two historical entry states. It did not include a new
external maintainer interview, a production installation over multiple tasks,
or a community capable of accepting, refusing, forking, and modifying the
method. The entry packets intentionally retained only governing instructions,
the attention-driven method, and goal/record state; missing design and code may
affect a proposed next implementation move, although both historical states had
the same evidence boundary and the tested mainline distinction lived in the
goal/record pair. The action-audit evaluator also inferred which anonymized
candidate was treated, so its `improved` method-delta classification was not
fully blind; the later ordinary-path probe independently overturned its direct
change recommendation. Those limits weaken broad adoption claims but do not
rescue the principle candidate: the matched mass-line treatment itself was
independently classified as overlap, and the action-level decision was
explained by existing P-IDs.
