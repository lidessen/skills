# P07 — Interpretation

**Sequence source:** P07｜由具体经抽象再上升为具体｜马克思主义哲学

> Read P07 in `principles/SEQUENCE.md` first.

## Shared reading

Begin with the actual concrete: the observed whole, its history, and the
relations that make the present question real. Analysis resolves it into the
determinations needed to explain it; judgment then returns to a concrete whole
in which those determinations are understood together. “Concrete” is therefore
a unity of relevant determinations, not a pile of detail or a premature
implementation plan.

## Decision questions

- Which observed forms and source links must remain available before abstracting?
- Which determinations are necessary to explain this concrete situation together?
- What relation would be lost if the proposed reconstitution stayed as separate
  principles, metrics, or renamed components?

## Boundary and common misreading

Abstraction is not arbitrary simplification, deletion of inconvenient history,
or relabelling a preference as an essence. It must remain answerable to the
concrete from which it came and return to a more intelligible concrete. Do not
demand exhaustive modelling before action: P15 can select a minimal move when
the available synthesis is sufficient for its hard constraints.

## Theoretical roots

- Karl Marx, *Grundrisse*, “The Method of Political Economy” — inquiry starts
  from the real and concrete, analytically reaches simpler determinations, and
  reconstructs the concrete in thought as their unity.

## Expression probes

- `reframe` extracts a domain's functions from inherited surface, then makes a
  new product shape concrete through new primitives.
- Skill regeneration inventories existing forms, derives a functional map, and
  composes only the retained functions into a target architecture.
- Source-preserving article refactoring separates reusable material from old
  rhetorical form before returning it to a new argument.

## Revision discipline

Revise from a failed expression probe, counterexample, or corrected source root.
Clarify when a concrete has been abstracted too early or reconstructed without
its governing relation. A new decision consequence beyond this movement requires
a sequence candidate rather than an expanded interpretation.
