# P16 — Interpretation

**Sequence source:** P16｜表达形式须使实践主体能够行动；形式错位，正确内容亦会失效｜辩证法 / 实践论

> Read P16 in `principles/SEQUENCE.md` first.

## Shared reading

Content is not effective merely because it is correct. Its form must let the
actual practitioner perceive, judge, and perform the required next action under
real conditions. The target action and subject therefore shape the expression.

## Decision questions

- Who must act from this artifact, and what concrete action must it enable?
- Does this form assume a system, tool, or knowledge that the actor does not have?

## Boundary and common misreading

An archival record, proof, or audit trail can optimize for fidelity rather than
immediate execution. P16 applies when an artifact is intended to shape action;
P11 decides who may verify or commit that action, and P13 decides whether its
result becomes shared fact.

## Theoretical roots

- V. I. Lenin, “Plan of Hegel’s Dialectics (Logic)” (1915) — content and form
  named as dialectical categories; this supplies a conceptual root, not this
  project’s full operational formulation.
- Project evidence: `principles/adopted/form-enables-action.md`, which
  records the Moniro feedback-loop rewrite from imagined system specification to
  agent-facing guide.

## Expression probes

- A `SKILL.md` gives executable guidance to an agent; reference material serves
  a different reader and action.
- An agent-facing CLI or MCP surface is tested by completing the operation, not
  by visual polish alone; P11/P13 still govern acceptance and fact admission.

## Revision discipline

Revise from a failed expression probe, counterexample, or corrected source root.
Clarify action, subject, and form. A new attention-allocation concern belongs to
P09 or P10 rather than expanding P16.
