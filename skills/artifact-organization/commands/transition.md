# Apply One Organization Transition

Apply one audited wave. Do not infer approval from the existence of a plan.

## Preconditions

- The audit names one material gap, one bounded wave, and an acceptance
  condition.
- The host's designated human approved changes to semantic authority, public
  index, or irreversible evidence.

## Steps

1. If durable coordination is needed, create or update the single campaign
   record described in [artifact-paths.md](../references/artifact-paths.md).
2. Execute non-destructive changes first. Move, retire, or merge durable paths
   only after their successor or archive is reachable.
3. Verify in the same pass:
   - **Gap closure:** the accepted target and actual carrier now agree.
   - **Authority:** every projection still names and follows its source.
   - **Inheritance:** a later actor can recover every reason needed for a future
     decision.
   - **Necessity:** every surviving process artifact and distinction changes a
     future response; otherwise remove or make it conditional.
4. Record `pass`, `blocked`, or `fail` with evidence. Authority or inheritance
   failure is `fail`.
5. On pass, promote stable organization into host design and move the campaign
   record to `sessions/` or the host archive when it remains useful evidence.
   Discard it when no later decision needs it. Do not begin another wave.

Never leave an active planning headquarters after the transition settles.
