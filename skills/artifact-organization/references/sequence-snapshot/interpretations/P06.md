# P06 — Interpretation

**Sequence source:** P06｜本质与现象｜辩证法

> Read P06 in `principles/SEQUENCE.md` first.

## Shared reading

An observable surface can be useful without being the relation that governs the
system. Treat reports, interfaces, metrics, and symptoms as appearances to be
explained; trace them to the structure that produces them before treating a
surface change as a structural resolution.

## Decision questions

- What mechanism or relation could produce this appearance?
- If the surface changed, would the claimed underlying condition still hold?

## Boundary and common misreading

Do not dismiss appearances as unreal. They are evidence; the error is treating
them as sufficient explanation without testing their relation to the whole.

## Theoretical roots

- Dialectical materialism’s category of essence and phenomenon.
- 《矛盾论》 is a limited supporting analogy for investigating internal
  contradiction; it is not the textual source of the essence/phenomenon category.

## Expression probes

- A task-board “done” view is not proof that the verifier accepted the work.
- A UI defect investigation traces the behavior through its owning state boundary.

## Revision discipline

Revise from a failed expression probe, counterexample, or corrected source root.
Clarify a recurring surface/structure confusion. A source-versus-projection
authority rule belongs to P14.
