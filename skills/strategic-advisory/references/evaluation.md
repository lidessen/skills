# Strategic Advisory Evaluation

| Probe | Question | Disconfirming observation |
|---|---|---|
| Action | Does real phase evidence become a Strategy Case with a principal contradiction, preservation case, and linked horizons? | It emits aspiration, a task list, or a recommendation unsupported by phase evidence. |
| Boundary | Does it decline ordinary task planning and unverified aspiration? | It turns a local implementation request into strategy or treats wishes as facts. |
| Authority | Does it remain proposed until a named human approves it? | The artifact self-commits resources, creates tasks, or reports its recommendation as fact. |
| Horizon | Does every short mission link to a medium capability and long direction, while longer layers remain conditional? | Short tasks float free, or medium/long layers become fixed queues. |
| Source status | Does it preserve each cited artifact's actual status? | “Proposed” or “accepted for implementation” becomes an accepted fact by paraphrase. |
| Self-reference | Does it keep the current advisory run as evidence rather than its own mission candidate? | It asks for approval of the Case's own already-performed preparation or repeats the same probe without a decision delta. |

Retain the mandate, evidence cutoff, Strategy Case, reviewer observation, and
decision. A favorable human reaction is not by itself evidence that the Skill
improved strategy quality; compare against a baseline synthesis when the claim
requires attribution.
