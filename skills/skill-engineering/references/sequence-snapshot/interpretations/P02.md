# P02 — Interpretation

**Sequence source:** P02｜实事求是｜唯物主义认识论

> Read P02 in `principles/SEQUENCE.md` first.

## Shared reading

Start from the actual object, conditions, and evidence—not from a desired
conclusion, a remembered pattern, or a prestigious theory. Theory organizes the
investigation; it cannot substitute for the object being investigated.

## Decision questions

- What is directly observed, and what is inference or preference?
- Which condition would make this conclusion no longer fit reality?

## Boundary and common misreading

Facts do not select their own meaning or action. P04 identifies the governing
contradiction; P03 and P08 test and revise the resulting judgment.

## Theoretical roots

- 毛泽东，《改造我们的学习》 — the formulation “实事求是”.
- 毛泽东，《实践论》 — knowledge begins in engagement with the objective world.

## Expression probes

- A design review opens source code and runtime evidence before recommending a
  structural change.
- A skill candidate cites independent observations rather than an attractive slogan.

## Revision discipline

Revise from a failed expression probe, counterexample, or corrected source root.
Clarify evidence and inference boundaries. A new fact-admission transition
belongs to P13 or a candidate, not an expansion of P02.
