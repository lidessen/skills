# P01 — Interpretation

**Sequence source:** P01｜原则高于规则｜一般化判断

> Read P01 in `principles/SEQUENCE.md` first. This is a shared reading, not a
> replacement definition.

## Shared reading

A rule compresses a past judgment; a principle preserves the reason that made
that judgment sound. In a new situation, recover the reason and judge again
rather than mechanically extending the old form.

## Decision questions

- Is this instruction a hard constraint, or a past solution being mistaken for one?
- What reason would remain if its familiar wording disappeared?

## Boundary and common misreading

This does not license ignoring explicit safety, legal, or acceptance constraints.
“Principles over rules” rejects blind formalism, not accountable discipline.

## Theoretical roots

- 《道德经》第一章 — language does not exhaust the way it points toward; an
  analogy for resisting fixation on a formulation, not a direct operational rule.
- 毛泽东，《实践论》 — Marxism as a guide to action rather than dogma.

## Expression probes

- A skill explains the judgment its steps serve and lets the agent adapt means.
- A review distinguishes a binding invariant from a checklist inherited from a
  different context.

## Revision discipline

Revise from a failed expression probe, counterexample, or corrected source root.
Clarify how to recover a rule’s reason. If a new kind of constraint or authority
is introduced, create a sequence candidate rather than redefining P01.
