# P05 — Interpretation

**Sequence source:** P05｜具体问题具体分析｜矛盾特殊性

> Read P05 in `principles/SEQUENCE.md` first.

## Shared reading

No general principle executes itself. Apply it through the actual history,
constraints, actors, and state of this case. The same contradiction can require
different forms of resolution when its concrete conditions differ.

## Decision questions

- Which conditions make this case unlike the precedent being invoked?
- What would a faithful application change here, rather than merely repeat?

## Boundary and common misreading

Specificity is not permission for arbitrary exceptions. P01 and P02 still
require a general reason and factual grounding; P08 applies when the application
makes an empirical claim that needs a checkable consequence.

## Theoretical roots

- 毛泽东，《矛盾论》 — the particularity of contradiction and concrete analysis.

## Expression probes

- A writing skill changes argument structure for the audience and thesis rather
  than applying one article template.
- A verifier chooses evidence appropriate to the system’s actual risk boundary.

## Revision discipline

Revise from a failed expression probe, counterexample, or corrected source root.
Clarify relevant case conditions. If they form a recurring new distinction,
propose a candidate rather than accumulating exceptions here.
