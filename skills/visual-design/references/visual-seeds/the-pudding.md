## Why retained

The collection permits each story to have a radically different palette,
illustration, interaction, and composition while a blunt editorial index keeps
the publication recognizable. Variation follows the argument and evidence
rather than one portable house treatment.

## Decision seed

- Ask whether coherence belongs in every page's surface or in the editorial
  frame, voice, standards, and way readers enter and leave individual works.
- Require a custom visual form to earn its existence by explaining or advancing
  the argument.
- Let a simple index carry orientation when the works themselves need broad
  expressive freedom.

## Fits and transfer boundary

Useful for visual journalism, essays, exhibitions, and portfolios of genuinely
different works. Transfer the relation between argument-specific form and a
stable editorial frame. The source's index treatment, story devices, palette,
type, and voice remain source-specific. A normal knowledge base rarely earns
one-off production per article.
