# Bike New York — A Reproducible Public Identity

## Live artifact

- [Bike New York identity, website, and campaign](https://www.pentagram.com/work/bike-new-york-2/story)

## Why retained

The identity joins immediate recognition, street-sign directness, and a mark
simple enough for riders to redraw. Hand-drawn variants and animation are
credible because reproducibility and public participation are present in the
mark's construction.

## Decision seed

- Ask whether an identity should be guarded as one perfect asset or remain
  recognizable while people reproduce and vary it.
- Derive expressive variation from a stable construction and participation
  relation rather than applying a hand-drawn filter after the fact.
- Test the mark with its real website, campaign, size, motion, and public-use
  conditions instead of accepting an isolated logo board.

## Fits and transfer boundary

Useful for civic, community, educational, and participatory identities. Do not
copy the source's mark construction, campaign vocabulary, treatments, or
variants. Transfer only the relation between stable recognition, reproducible
construction, and public participation. The source remains a third-party case
study; observation does not grant reuse rights.
