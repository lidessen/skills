# Excalidraw — Provisional Marks as a Working Surface

## Live artifacts

- [Open drawing surface](https://excalidraw.com/)
- [Product presentation](https://plus.excalidraw.com/)

## Why retained

The hand-drawn language is not merely friendly decoration: it lowers the cost
of making incomplete thoughts visible and keeps attention on structure rather
than pixel polish. The product presentation extends that relation through
marks, annotations, headings, and examples while its controls remain precise.

## Decision seed

- Ask whether visible provisionality helps people contribute, revise, or discuss
  work earlier.
- Concentrate expressive imperfection in user-created content and identity while
  preserving predictable operational controls.
- Judge success by whether people communicate structure faster, not by whether
  every element resembles a sketch.

## Fits and transfer boundary

Useful for collaborative thinking, diagrams, early planning, education, and
tools where editability matters more than finish. Transfer the relation between
visible provisionality, early contribution, and precise operation—not the
source's drawing vocabulary, type, canvas, or treatments. Expressive
provisionality can reduce trust or legibility in settled records and dense
operations.
