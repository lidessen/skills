# Maggie Appleton — Authored Knowledge with Visible Status

## Live artifacts

- [Home and essays](https://maggieappleton.com/)
- [Tools for Thought as Cultural Practices](https://maggieappleton.com/tools-for-thought)

## Why retained

The site holds long-form intellectual authority and personal authorship
together. Editorial scale, differentiated content status, linking, and
occasional illustration keep unlike knowledge roles legible without flattening
them into a uniform feed.

## Decision seed

- Ask whether the project has materially different epistemic states that should
  remain visible rather than receive one publication treatment.
- Let typography, metadata, linking, and occasional illustration carry
  authorship without making every paragraph expressive.
- Differentiate essays, notes, patterns, and library material by epistemic role
  while keeping them inside one recognizable authored collection.

## Fits and transfer boundary

Useful for personal research, visual essays, and collections whose authorial
voice matters. Transfer differentiated epistemic status and authorial presence.
The source's identity vocabulary, status taxonomy, and treatments remain
source-specific unless the host project's own content model independently earns
an equivalent decision. Reinspect the live site; this card preserves an
observation, not its current pixels.
