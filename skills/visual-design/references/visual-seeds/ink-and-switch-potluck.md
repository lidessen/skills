# Ink & Switch Potluck — Documents Gradually Becoming Software

## Live artifact

- [Potluck: Dynamic documents as personal software](https://www.inkandswitch.com/potluck/)

## Why retained

The essay keeps a calm research-paper reading form while introducing diagrams,
margin notes, live examples, code, and interactive prototypes only when the
argument needs them. The document remains continuous as explanation becomes
demonstration and then executable material.

## Decision seed

- Ask whether interaction should replace a document or gradually enrich its
  existing reading structure.
- Keep primary prose stable and let examples, annotations, and prototypes enter
  at the point where static explanation stops being sufficient.
- Use margins and secondary columns for authorship, provenance, and local
  explanation without turning every page into a dashboard.

## Fits and transfer boundary

Useful for research reports, technical essays, explainable tools, and documents
with executable evidence. Transfer the relation between a stable reading path
and locally earned interaction. The source's document treatment, annotation
grammar, demonstration frames, and tone remain source-specific. Restraint is
part of this seed.
