## Why retained

The publication's visual identity is inseparable from its solar hosting and
bandwidth limits. Dithered, category-tinted images, default type, visible page
weight and battery state, and a direct editorial grid make infrastructure
legible while remaining visually distinctive.

## Decision seed

- Ask whether a material or operational condition should become part of the
  reader's understanding rather than remain invisible implementation detail.
- Let one real constraint generate image, type, color, availability, and
  identity decisions together.
- Preserve the right to revise a signature element when practice shows that it
  obstructs reading or factual interpretation.

## Fits and transfer boundary

Useful for publications whose production conditions are ethically or
conceptually meaningful. Transfer the causal relation between an actual
infrastructure constraint, reader understanding, and revisable publication
form. The source's image process, status indicators, typography, palette, and
identity remain source-specific; without the governing material relation they
become costume.
