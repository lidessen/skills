# Visual Storytelling

## Use for

Choosing a website or document form from its audience, argument, evidence, and
narrative rather than from a style label; deciding when a custom visual or
interactive treatment is essential; and testing whether visual expression
clarifies the story.

## Decision lenses

- [The Pudding's design process](https://pudding.cool/process/how-to-make-dope-shit-part-2/)
  treats admired work and repeated drafts as material for solving a particular
  visual problem, not as a look to copy.
- [The Pudding's storytelling process](https://pudding.cool/process/how-to-make-dope-shit-part-3/)
  begins from audience, purpose, complexity, narrative structure, and evidence
  transparency before choosing a visual essay form.
- [Continue, Pivot, or Put It Down](https://pudding.cool/process/pivot-continue-down/)
  preserves the legitimacy of changing or abandoning an attractive direction
  when evidence and practice do not support it.

## Transfer boundary

Recover audience-and-thesis-led form, storyboard practice, visual necessity,
iteration, and transparent evidence. Do not default to scrollytelling, custom
data graphics, playful editorial voice, or one-off page structures when stable
reading or operational interaction better serves the content.
