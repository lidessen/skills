# Identity Variation

## Use for

Relating a logo or recurring mark to product surfaces; deciding what remains
recognizable while expression changes; and testing whether variation expresses
a real project relation rather than applying a filter.

## Decision lenses

- [Evolving the Google identity](https://design.google/library/evolving-google-identity)
  separates a logotype, compact mark, and dynamic dots by context while
  preserving a recognizable family. It shows that size, neighboring elements,
  optical behavior, and interactive state can require different expressions.
- [Bike New York](https://www.pentagram.com/work/bike-new-york-2/story)
  connects the mark's easy-to-draw construction and hand-drawn variations to
  participation and public accessibility. The hand-made quality follows from
  the organization's relation to riders rather than from a generic doodle cue.

## Transfer boundary

Recover invariant-versus-variation reasoning, consequential-context tests, and
the link between construction and participation. Do not copy letter tricks,
primary colors, stencil forms, bicycle imagery, hand-drawn animation, or a
multi-mark architecture without equivalent identity and consumer conditions.
